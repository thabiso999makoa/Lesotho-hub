Farming Tips

Crop Farming

  • Rotate crops each season to maintain soil fertility.
  • Use compost and organic fertilizers to improve yields.
  • Water crops early in the morning or late afternoon to reduce evaporation.

Livestock Farming

  • Provide clean water and balanced feed daily.
  • Maintain proper housing to protect animals from harsh weather.
  • Vaccinate regularly to prevent diseases.

Poultry Farming

  • Ensure poultry houses are well-ventilated and dry.
  • Feed chickens with protein-rich food for better egg production.
  • Keep nests clean to avoid contamination of eggs.

Dairy Farming

  • Provide cows with nutrient-rich feed for quality milk production.
  • Milk cows at the same time each day to maintain consistency.
  • Keep milking equipment and storage containers clean to ensure hygiene.
  • Give cows access to clean water at all times.
